My Work

Project 1

As part of my coursework with Udacity, I designed, tested, and iterated on a mobile VR experience called Puzzler. In this game, the player enters a dungeon with orbs generated on the inside. The player is then asked to solve a puzzle similar to a 3D Simon Says game in order to leave the dungeon and win the game.

Project 2

This is the 5th and final project for the “core” tracks in the Udacity VR Development Nanodegree program. For this project, the student is required to research a topic related to a VR company. VR technology, or an industry that's could be impacted by VR. The student then creates a “night at the museum” experience. This involves a museum-like environment that has multiple information booths which include information on the topic. My topic -- foveated rendering.

Project 3

I created an augmented reality scene for Android using Unity and Vuforia. It uses extended tracking with user defined functionality, which allows the user to define their own targets to build the scene onto. In this case, I define a DVD case as my target using the camera icon to scan the image of the DVD case. After scanning, the scene is built,l and I can fire bullets at the imperial walkers.
